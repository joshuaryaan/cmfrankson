Channa-Mae Frankson

Bachelor of Architectural Studies, Majoring in Interior Architecture
Postgraduate Diploma in Interior Architecture

Concerned with matters of the senses in perception, experiential design and spatial narrative, the works interweave techniques and ideals of interior architecture and the fine arts. Driven by curiosity, the processes are multi-facetted yet maintain a sense of order and logic within the establishment of a design language.

Current muses include (but are not limited to):
Gaston Bachelard, Agnes Martin, Max Lamb, Tony Smith.
